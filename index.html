Tesla Dogs collection is available on GetGems platform which is the biggest NFT marketplace on The Open Network (TON) blockchain.
The main currency on the TON blockchain is Toncoin. The only thing you need to buy NFT on GetGems is to have a cryptowallet and have enough Toncoins on it. Then you just click the button “Buy Now” near NFT image and follow the instructions that the platform will offer to you.

We highly recommend you to use Tonkeeper wallet for keeping your NFTs and Toncoins.
how to buy toncoin?
You can buy Toncoin easily using Tonkeeper interface. First of all you have to download the application and create a new wallet. The Tonkeeper will ask you to remember 24 keywords. Write them down carefully! These 24 keywords are the only way to get access to your wallet.
When you created your own wallet touch the “Buy” button under Toncoin price chart. These exchanges - Neocrypto, Mercuryo, Wallet - will allow you to buy Toncoin using a credit card. Please, choose any exchange and follow the instructions that it will offer to you.
